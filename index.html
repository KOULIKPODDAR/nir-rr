Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

  • What is the admission process?
    The admission process is based on your performance in national-level entrance exams like JEE Main for B.Tech and GATE for M.Tech.
  • Is there any hostel facility?
    Yes, NIT Raipur provides hostel accommodations for both male and female students with all necessary amenities.
  • What are the scholarship opportunities?
    The institute offers various scholarships based on academic performance, economic background, and other criteria.
  • Can I apply for more than one course?
    No, you can apply for only one course during the admission process.
